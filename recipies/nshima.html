Nshima

Ingrediates of Ubwali AKA Nshima

    Melie Meal
    Water

Things to Have before starting to cook Nshima

    1. Impoto: Cooking Pot
    2. Umwinko: Cooking stick
    3. Amenshi: Water
    4. Umulilo: Stove, Fire Braziare, Gas Sotove
    5. Ubunga: Melie Meal

Procedure

The most important thing to consider when making Ubwali is the amount of Water and the level of Fire. This is important beacuse less fire makes the Nshima not to be fully prepared.

The other important thing is the amount of Water been used to make the Nshima this is countend depending on te number of people and the level of Melie meal you have

Steps to take

    Step 1

Put water in Impoto ( The Cooking Pot)

Wait for about 15 minutes for the Water to boil.

    Step 2

Add Millie

Contiue Stiring using a Cooking stick (Umwinko

Wait until the Solution is Solid: (Not solid in the form of a rock

    Step 3

Wait for about 15 Minutes

Continue Stiring in the Solution

Cover the Cooking pot: Impoto for more than 30 minutes

This step is important because it allows the Solution to be mixed thougly and effectively so that all the Millie meal is ok

    Step 4

This is a final and last Step

After 15 - 30 minutes of allowing to boil as a Solution now start adding more Melie Meal wilest continuening to stir in the cooking pot to make the solution more solid

Adding more Melie Meal to make the solute more hard to make into nshima

After 10 - 20 Minutes of adding Melie Meal the Nshima Now Should be in a Solution Suitable to eat

    Are You Done?
